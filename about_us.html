Travel with Confidence At SoloStride

we aim to make solo travel accessible for individuals with disabilities. Our platform connects you with local helpers to assist with everything from mobility support to luggage handling, ensuring that your travel experience is as smooth and independent as possible.

Our Mission

At SoloStride, our mission is to empower solo travelers with disabilities by providing accessible, on-demand assistance to ensure their journeys are safe, independent, and Effortless and enjoyable

Our Vission

We envision a world where every individual, regardless of physical ability, can travel with confidence. Through innovation and support, we aim to break down barriers and promote inclusivity in every travel experience.
